leading-relaxed my-4">interface ai-inference {</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">record tensor {</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">data: list<f32>,</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">shape: list<u32>,</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">}</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">run-inference: func(input: tensor) -> tensor;</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">get-model-info: func() -> model-info;</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">}</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">world ai-platform {</p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4"><code class='bg-[var(--color-surface)] text-[var(--color-accent)] px-1.5 py-0.5 rounded text-sm font-mono border border-[var(--color-border)]'>`</code></p><p class="text-[var(--color-text-2)] leading-relaxed my-4">这意味着：</p><ul class='space-y-2 my-5 ml-4 list-disc'><li class="text-[var(--color-text-2)]
